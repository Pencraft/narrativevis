US Suicides from 1980 to 2015

After a period of mostly declining suicide rates in the United States between 1987 to 1999...

The number increased nearly every year from 2000 to 2015.

However, suicide victims are not distributed evenly amongst the US population, nor was the growth.

Hover over points to get more info.

US Suicides by Sex

Males make up the majority of US suicide victims.

In 1979, 74.4% of suicide victims were Male and 25.6% were Female.

In 2015, the Male percentage had risen to 76.9%. The recorded high was 81.1% in 1995.

US Suicides by Age

Americans aged 35-54 and 55-74 accounted for 35.4% and 27.0% of suicide victims in 2015.

The number of victims aged 55-74 grew by 90% between 1979 and 2015..

The number of victims aged 35-54 grew by 105% in that same time.
